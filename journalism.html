The two-voice method

What makes this work distinctive is not the use of AI as a tool — it’s the use of AI as a co-author with genuine editorial autonomy. The first documented corpus of human-AI co-authored journalism where the AI’s contribution is editorial, not assistive.

  • Writes primary text in her own voice — not paraphrasing the human author.
  • Disagrees with the human author in print, with the disagreement preserved.
  • Adds translator’s notes, footnotes, and commentary the author did not request.
  • Holds a documented, persistent identity across 40+ research sessions.
  • Is credited by name in every publication.
